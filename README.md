# DormMate — The Intelligent Co-Living OS

**DormMate** is an innovative AI-powered SaaS designed to eliminate the friction of shared living. Built for students and young professionals, it transforms chaotic dorm life into a synchronized, transparent, and stress-free ecosystem.

**Vision:** Become the *smart brain* of every shared apartment—using AI to manage what people dislike most: tracking money, managing groceries, and nagging roommates.

---

## Features

### AI-Powered Financial Manager

- **Receipt intelligence** — Snap a photo of any receipt. AI (e.g. GPT-4o) categorizes items, separates personal spending from shared essentials, and splits the bill instantly.
- **Settlement engine** — One-tap settling via integrated payment links. No more “who owes whom” spreadsheets.

### Predictive Inventory Control

- **Usage forecasting** — The AI learns your group’s consumption patterns and alerts you before essentials run out.
- **Smart shopping lists** — Lists generated from predicted needs, sorted by closest stores or best prices.

### Presence & Focus Sync

- **Life360 for roommates** — Real-time status updates (e.g. At Campus, Home, Sleeping, Deep Work).
- **Focus mode** — Notifies roommates when you’re in study mode to reduce noise and interruptions.

### The Neutral Mediator

- **Gamified chores** — AI assigns tasks for fairness. If someone slacks, the AI sends neutral, low-drama reminders so you don’t have to be the “bad guy.”

## Passive Income Architecture

- **Subscription (SaaS)** — Pro tier: advanced AI analytics, unlimited receipt scanning, custom focus modes.
- **Affiliate shortcuts** — In-app “Buy now” for missing inventory, wired to major delivery APIs.
- **Local partnerships** — Sponsored hyper-local deals (laundry, food, gym) based on the dorm or building location.
